Hello, I'm Dilki Wathsala Attanayake

I'm a passionate full-stack developer. I have a strong drive to create innovative solutions that make a positive impact on the world. With a background in computer science and a deep understanding of both client-side and server-side development.

I have hands-on experience in a variety of programming languages, including Java, Python, OOP, React, Tailwind CSS, Node.js and database management. My journey in tech has allowed me to work on exciting projects, from crafting interactive websites to building server-side architectures, each one pushing my skills to new heights.

Currently, I am the group leader of my university's software development group project, coastalcanopy.org.lk, a comprehensive, community-oriented web application dedicated to the monitoring, protection, and sustainable management of mangrove ecosystems in Sri Lanka. Leading this project has strengthened my leadership, problem-solving, and project management skills while fostering teamwork and innovation among my team members.
